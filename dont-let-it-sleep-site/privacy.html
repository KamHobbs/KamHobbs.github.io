Don’t Let It Sleep Privacy Policy

```

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Don’t Let It Sleep handles information when you use the app.

No Account Sign-In

Don’t Let It Sleep does not require you to create an account or sign in with a username and password.

Game Center

Don’t Let It Sleep uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. If you use Game Center, Apple may process information associated with your Game Center account according to Apple’s privacy policies and your Apple account settings.

iCloud and CloudKit

Don’t Let It Sleep uses Apple iCloud/CloudKit to store private game-related data, such as Wake Token balance information, reward status, and related ledger records. This data is associated with your Apple/iCloud account and is used to keep your game state accurate and persistent.

In-App Purchases

Don’t Let It Sleep offers optional in-app purchases through Apple, including Wake Token packs, a one-time Starter Pack, and Remove Ads. Purchases are processed by Apple. Don’t Let It Sleep does not receive or store your payment card information.

Advertising

Don’t Let It Sleep uses Google AdMob to provide advertising. Ads do not appear during gameplay. The app may offer optional rewarded ads that can grant a Wake Token after completion, and interstitial ads may appear outside gameplay at natural transition points.

Google AdMob may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, usage data, and other information as described in Google’s policies and based on your device privacy settings.

Diagnostics and Crash Data

Apple may provide developers with crash logs, performance information, and diagnostics if you have chosen to share analytics with app developers in your device settings. This information is used to improve app stability and performance.

Support Requests

If you contact support by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message, such as your device model, iOS version, app version, or description of an issue. This information is used only to respond to your request and provide support.

Children’s Privacy

Don’t Let It Sleep does not require account creation and does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. The app may use Apple services and Google AdMob as described above.

Data Sharing

Don’t Let It Sleep does not sell your personal information. The app relies on Apple services for Game Center, iCloud/CloudKit, and in-app purchases, and Google AdMob for advertising.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact: dontletitsleepapp@gmail.com
```
