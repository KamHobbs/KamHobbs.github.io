Don’t Let It Sleep

A quick-reaction timing game about staying awake, pushing your luck, and tapping before it is too late.

Don’t Let It Sleep is a fast, simple timing game where every second matters. Wait for the screen to dim, tap at the last possible moment, and build your score without tapping too early or letting the screen fall asleep.

Earn Wake Tokens, use Emergency Wake when a run is on the line, chase Miracle taps, and compete through Game Center leaderboards and achievements.

The game includes optional Wake Token purchases, optional rewarded ads, and no ads during gameplay.
