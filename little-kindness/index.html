How It Works

1. Open the app

Little Kindness previews a positive quote right on the main screen.

2. Tap Send

The app selects a random contact and prepares a message with a quote.

3. Confirm and send

You review the message in the system composer and decide whether to send it.

Why Little Kindness?

Simple by design

Little Kindness is intentionally lightweight. No complicated setup. No social feed. No clutter. Just one gentle idea: send something kind.

A small gesture can matter

Sometimes the right message at the right moment can make someone feel remembered, encouraged, or loved. Little Kindness helps make that easy.

Features

  • Optional daily reminder
