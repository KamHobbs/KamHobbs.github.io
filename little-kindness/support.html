Frequently Asked Questions

How does Little Kindness work?

Little Kindness previews a positive quote on the main screen. When you tap Send, the app picks a random contact and opens a pre-filled message composer. You review the message before anything is sent.

Does the app send messages automatically?

No. Little Kindness prepares the message for you, but you confirm before it sends.

Why does the app need access to Contacts?

The app uses your contacts to choose a random recipient for your message. Contacts access is managed by iOS and Android system permissions, and you can change that access at any time in Settings.

Why does the app ask for notification permission?

If you enable reminders, Little Kindness can send a gentle daily reminder to open the app and share a little kindness.

How do I change reminder settings?

Open the Settings screen inside the app to turn the daily reminder on or off and change the reminder time.

How do I change Contacts access?

Use the Settings screen inside the app. Depending on your current permission state and platform, Little Kindness will either request access, show the selected-contacts picker, or open the device Settings page for the app.
